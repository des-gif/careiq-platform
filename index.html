Full briefing

The extended treatment.

The complete picture for providers, partners and investors — what it is, why it matters, and the commercial case.

What it is

A care compliance and inspection intelligence platform — not just a folder

CareIQ is a digital compliance, audit and inspection-readiness system for care providers who need stronger oversight, better evidence, safer practice and clearer preparation for Ofsted and CQC inspection. It brings inspection readiness, audit tools, restraint and de-escalation recording, safer recruitment, Right to Work checks, investigation reviews, policy alerts, report generation, evidence folders, service profiles, student tracking and regulatory learning into one place.

It gives managers, Responsible Individuals, Nominated Individuals, directors and QA leads a structured system for seeing what is happening, what is missing, what needs action and what evidence is available — moving services from reactive compliance to active oversight.

Why it is investable

A regulation-led market that must evidence everything

The care sector is regulation-led. Providers must evidence safe practice, governance, safeguarding, staffing, training, audit activity and management oversight — creating clear, recurring demand for practical tools that keep services organised and inspection-ready.

  • ✓Regulation-led, recurring demand across children's and adult services
  • ✓Subscription revenue with consultant and training-provider licensing
  • ✓Anchors a suite — Inspector Coach AI and the Restraint Toolkit plug in
  • ✓Sells to single services, groups, consultants and training organisations
  • ✓Founder has direct sector credibility and inspection experience
